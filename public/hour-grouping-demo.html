Hour grouping options — demo

Compare six ways to show AWST hour blocks on the flight board. Same mock flights in every section; hour buckets use estimated time (fallback scheduled). Orange line = “now” divider (fixed at 2:25 PM AWST for this demo). Open via http://localhost:3000/hour-grouping-demo.html or this file directly.

  • Option 3 (hour band headers) is the most screen-reader friendly.
  • Stripes and dots use distinct hues — not the only cue if you ship 2, 4, or 6.
  • The now-divider is independent of hour grouping in all variants.
